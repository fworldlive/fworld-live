User Conduct Guidelines

Welcome to Fworld Live. Our mission is to offer the best voice party and talent-based platform where creators and users can interact in a safe, respectful, and vibrant environment. To ensure this, we ask all our users to contribute positively and respectfully to our growing global community.

To preserve the integrity of our platform, Fworld Live reserves the right to suspend or terminate any account that violates our guidelines or engages in harmful behavior. Suspension decisions are final and may not always include an explanation, though users are welcome to appeal.

In addition to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, these User Conduct Guidelines outline acceptable behavior on the Fworld Live platform, which includes all voice rooms, chats, profile details, usernames, and other activities. Violations may lead to content removal, account suspension, or permanent bans. These guidelines are subject to change to reflect the evolution of our services.

Complying with Laws

You must abide by all applicable local, national, and international laws. Any content or behavior promoting illegal activity is strictly prohibited.

Ban Evasion

Users attempting to evade suspension by creating new accounts or using alternate profiles will be permanently banned.

Personal Safety

Dangerous behavior is not tolerated. This includes:

  • Drug and alcohol abuse
  • Weapon use or display
  • Promotion of terrorism or extremism
  • Violence, vandalism, or reckless actions
  • Self-harm, suicide threats
  • Child abuse, exploitation, or grooming

Harassment and Threats

We have zero tolerance for:

  • Threatening or attempting to harm others
  • Brandishing weapons
  • Harassment or stalking of users or staff
  • Defamation and coordinated raids

Privacy Violations

Do not share personal information without consent. Prohibited actions include:

  • Sharing real names, addresses, ID numbers
  • Broadcasting private conversations or private property without permission
  • Posting restricted social media content

Hate Speech

Any form of discrimination, hate speech, or harassment based on race, gender, sexual orientation, disability, age, religion, or nationality is strictly prohibited.

Impersonation

You may not impersonate another person, especially Fworld Live staff or admins. This results in an immediate ban.

Scams and Fraud

We prohibit any attempt to scam or defraud users, including:

  • Fake accounts pretending to be Fworld Live staff
  • Attempts to steal personal info like OTPs, passwords, or IDs
  • Phishing links or messages

Security Tips:

  • Fworld Live will never ask for your password via DM
  • Never share sensitive info or click suspicious links

Spam and Malicious Behavior

Forbidden actions include:

  • Repetitive spamming
  • Phishing attempts
  • Distribution of malware or viruses
  • Fake engagement (likes, followers)
  • Selling or sharing user accounts
  • Reselling Fworld Live features (coins, VIP, etc.)

Sexually Explicit Content

Fworld Live is a safe space. We prohibit:

  • Pornography or explicit content
  • Sexually suggestive audio, emoji, or chat
  • Offering or requesting adult services

Child Safety

Any content or behavior endangering minors is strictly prohibited and will be reported to authorities. This includes:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
  • Grooming or exploitation
  • Sextortion or trafficking

Inappropriate Attire or Behavior

Nudity, sexual gestures, or revealing clothing is not allowed.

Extreme Violence and Gore

Content focused on excessive violence, blood, or gore is not allowed.

Copyright Violations

Do not upload or stream content you do not own or have rights to use. Examples include:

  • Broadcasting someone else’s stream
  • Playing unauthorized movies, shows, or matches
  • Using third-party copyrighted content without permission

Need Help or Want to Report?

If you experience or witness a violation of these guidelines, please contact our in-app support or email: Support@fworld.live

Your safety and positive experience are our priority.
